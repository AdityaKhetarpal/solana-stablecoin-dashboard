Where this data comes from
Supply & Composition
DefiLlama's stablecoin API tracks every stablecoin's supply on each blockchain daily. We pull Solana's total and break it down by asset (USDC, USDT, USDG, etc.).
Peg Health
Each stablecoin should trade at exactly $1.00. DefiLlama reports the current market price - deviations beyond ±0.5% are flagged as "Watch".
SOL Price (90 days)
Pulled from DefiLlama's coin price history, which aggregates on-chain and market data. No API key needed.
DeFi TVL (Total Value Locked)
The total USD value locked in Solana's DeFi protocols (Kamino, Drift, Orca, etc.), tracked daily by DefiLlama.
Chain Comparison
DefiLlama aggregates stablecoin supply across all major blockchains. This shows where Solana ranks globally right now.
Top Protocols by TVL
Live stablecoin TVL per protocol pulled directly from DefiLlama. Covers Kamino, Drift, Marginfi, Orca, and Jupiter — Solana's largest stablecoin venues.
Fear & Greed Index
The Crypto Fear & Greed Index from alternative.me, scored 0–100. Aggregates volatility, momentum, social sentiment, and dominance signals. Updated daily.
SOL 24h Change
Derived from DefiLlama's SOL price history — compares the most recent price to the previous day's close to show the 24-hour percentage move.
