Solana Stablecoin
Report — Q1 2026

On-chain analysis of stablecoin supply, transaction volume, DeFi TVL, and market composition on Solana. Jan 1 – Mar 31, 2026.

Big Idea

Solana stablecoins grew 20% while SOL fell 41% — the ecosystem now runs on infrastructure, not speculation.

Written for DeFi traders, protocol teams, and researchers tracking Solana's stablecoin ecosystem.
01 — Supply

Structural resilience

Total supply held above $15.7B even through SOL's steepest drawdown — stablecoin demand is now decoupled from token price.

02 — Volume

Record shattered in February

$650B in txn volume — 2× the previous all-chain monthly record. Solana's USDC volume surpassed Ethereum's for the first time.

03 — Structure

Yield-bearing crosses 12%

USDe/sUSDe and USDY collectively crossed 12% of supply, up from near zero a year ago. A regime change, not a blip.

Key takeaway
Supply held above $15.7B even as SOL crashed 41% from its January peak. The divergence began Feb 17 and widened through March — stablecoin users are treating Solana as infrastructure, not a speculative position.
